About Me

Hola! I am a passionate and enthusiastic graphic and web designer. I design and develop visual communication concepts, from a logo to a web page.

My tools of choice are Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign from the Adobe Suite. And Sublime + Git when developing websites with HTML5 and CSS3.

I am from Spain but I currently live in Norway, where I finished my Bachelor Degree in Advertising and Public Relations.

When I am not moving pixels around, I like to walk and discover new places, taking pictures and learning Norwegian.

If you want to know more about me please check out my full CV. And don't hesitate to get in contact even if you just want to say "hello"!
